By far the easiest way to make ramen. There's no doubt you will always want to make ramen like this whenever you're in a time crunch. And no one is in more time crunches than a hardworking college student. Learn how to make ramen THE COLLEGE WAY.

  1. Unwrap ramen and place into a bowl.
  2. Fill bowl up with water just above the ramen.
  3. Place bowl into microwave and heat or 1.5 minutes.
  4. Stir and repeat for another 1.5 minutes.
  5. Take out of microwave and mix in any seasonings desired.
  6. Lit it sit for a few minutes then enjoy.
